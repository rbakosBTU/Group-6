Business Development Manager

Our mission at CHARGERS is to develop the best product in the world and make it universally available. But we’ve got more left to do — and that's where you come in! CHARGERS is one of the most popular locally used product, with over 5000 users. We are passionate about educating our users, making fact-based decisions, and finding innovative solutions to complex problems. We offer meaningful work, limitless opportunities, and collaboration with world-class minds. Come brighten your life joining us! Drive business development and partnerships for CHARGERS. We’re seeking a business development leader to take a strategic, structured and proactive approach to growing our business through partnerships across markets and verticals. Join us to increase access to effectiveness by strategic partnerships.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and communicate an effective strategy for partnerships
  • Build compelling and quantifiable business cases for which partnerships to purse
  • Negotiate and execute partnerships to grow CHARGERS’s users and subscription business globally
  • Manage our most important partners across verticals (app stores, telcos, payment providers, brand partners, etc.)
  • Pursue high impact partnership opportunities across content, marketing, and other areas
  • Explore expansion of CHARGERS to new markets and platforms

Requirements:

  • Demonstrated track record of achievement in fast paced entrepreneurial work environments
  • 2+ years relevant work experience in across consulting, banking or business development
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Excellent analytical skills
  • Experience working closely with strategy, product and engineering colleagues
  • MBA from reputable university preferred
  • We will do everything we can within reason to make sure that your interview takes place in an environment that fairly and accurately assesses your skills. If you need assistance or accommodation, please contact your recruiter. We aim to return to office, and as such are requiring all employees to be fully vaccinated against COVID-19.
