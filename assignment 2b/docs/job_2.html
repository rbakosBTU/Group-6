Process Engineer Mixing (Electrode), Battery Cell

Our mission at CHARGERS is to develop the best product in the world and make it universally available. But we’ve got more left to do — and that's where you come in! CHARGERS is one of the most popular locally used product, with over 5000 users. We are passionate about educating our users, making fact-based decisions, and finding innovative solutions to complex problems. We offer meaningful work, limitless opportunities, and collaboration with world-class minds. Come brighten your life joining us! The ideal candidate takes pride in her/his hands-on work, analytical ability, organizational skills and attention to detail. She/He appreciates an environment where superior work is encouraged, noticed and rewarded and where individuals carry tremendous responsibility. If you are highly motivated to bring in your skills into the future of mobility than just apply, even if you never built batteries.

Responsibilities:

  • Monitor and reduce process variation using techniques such as Statistical Process Control (SPC) and Measurement Systems Analysis (MSA)
  • Participate in the development and maintenance of FMEAs and Control Plans
  • Develop and train Process technicians
  • Assist in the training of associates as needed
  • Create and maintain Standard Work Instructions for assigned processes
  • Monitor and audit manufacturing processes to ensure product specifications and standards are achieved

Requirements:

  • Bachelor or Master in any Engineering or Chemical discipline. Alternatively, the equivalent in experience and evidence of exceptional ability
  • Experience in a technology-based industry, e.g. food, cosmetics, pharmaceutic, cells, battery, semiconductor, clean & dry room, powder mixing, mixing, chemicals, concrete, building/construction materials, plastics, plastic polymers would be an advantage
  • Must possess process engineering skills (process development/improvement, troubleshooting, data analysis, constraint analysis, flow optimization, root cause analysis, etc.)
  • Strong team working skills at all levels of an organization, especially skilled at working with direct labor to understand challenges and work on developing optimal solutions using structured methods
  • Capable of identifying critical process parameters and applying statistics to process measurement and control
  • Comfortable providing clear and focused technical guidance to outside engineering resources
  • Hands-on mentality
  • German or English language skills required
  • We will do everything we can within reason to make sure that your interview takes place in an environment that fairly and accurately assesses your skills. If you need assistance or accommodation, please contact your recruiter. We aim to return to office, and as such are requiring all employees to be fully vaccinated against COVID-19.
